How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests. Instead, we spend weeks reading thousands of owner reviews across multiple retailers, forums like r/flashlight, and published reviews from trusted outdoor media. We track what components actually fail over time – switches, battery contacts, waterproofing – to separate marketing from durability. We also note which models have been consistently recommended by the community for years, not just the latest launch. Our recommendations prioritize real-world reliability over peak specs. A headlamp that dims after an hour or breaks after a few drops isn’t worth your money no matter how bright it is. We weight long-term owner feedback heavily and avoid models with frequent complaints about switch failure or corrosion.
