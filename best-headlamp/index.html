A good headlamp turns any task from frustrating to effortless, whether you are fumbling with a tent pole after dark, jogging a pre-dawn trail, or fixing a pipe under the sink. Battery type matters, too, rechargeable packs save money long-term, but standard AAA batteries are convenient on extended trips.
